Lost Ark — Hell Reward Picker

Compare gold value of every chest at every floor. Toggle Wealth +1 on chests to apply the 10× multiplier.

Changelog
May 29, 2026
Special Hones are now valued per key tier — the 1640 / 1680 / 1700 keys drop the Circulated Leapstone while the 1730 key drops the Transferred Leapstone, which are worth very different amounts of gold. The price panel now shows which one it's valuing.

Added an NA-East / EU-Central market region toggle.

Added a Destiny Shards price input with an Ignore Shards toggle, and corrected the per-floor base-shard counts.

Special Hones value now uses a Maxroll-accurate honing model and updates live as you edit prices. Added Stone & Leapstone T4 → T4.5 trade-up logic (uses the cheaper of buying T4.5 directly vs. trading up 5× T4) with a “market buy” highlight. Fusions and Lava's / Glacier's Breath got their own price inputs. Default unit prices now reflect recent auction-house data and refresh on their own.
April 30, 2026
Added material icons and tier-aware T4 / T4.5 material swapping, the Sands → Keys tab with a live chain gain/loss %, auction-house bundle pricing (×100) for stones, and set the default floor to 50–59 (the peak Blue Hell Key rate).
April 2026 — initial release
Chest-value picker, Cubes payout tab, and the Wealth +1 (10× base-reward) toggle.
